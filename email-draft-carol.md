Here's a brief overview of what I'd like to show:

**Question creation (fully customizable)**

- **Subjects:** Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, English, Computer Science, Arts, Physical Education, Languages, Business & Economics, Technology, and more, with sub-subjects (e.g. Algebra, Physics, History).
- **Grades:** K–12 plus Undergraduate and Graduate.

**My Questions library (manage, edit, export)**

- All created questions are saved in a **personal library** with **filters** by question type, grade, difficulty, and Bloom's level.
- Every question is **fully editable** (text, options, correct answer, explanation); MCQ options can be added or removed.
- **Bulk select** and **export to CSV** (including question text, type, options, correct answer, explanation, image URL, subject, topic, grade, difficulty) for use in other tools or print.
- **Per-question image management:** Generate or change images, choose placement and style.

The whole flow—from creation criteria to editing and export—is designed to be **fully customizable** so educators can align content with their curriculum and standards.

With Instaku, educators don't have to worry about writing prompts for different subjects and topics, fixing editing errors, or dealing with AI hallucinations—structured prompts, validation, and optional reference content (PDF or paste) take care of that. They don't have to worry about messy or inconsistent output: every question is parsed, validated, and stored in a consistent format (type, options, correct answer, explanation), and every question is fully editable (text, options, key, explanation) before export or use. They set the criteria; we deliver consistent, editable questions ready for their classroom or export.
